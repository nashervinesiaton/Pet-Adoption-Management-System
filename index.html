Our Mission: Healthy Pets, Happy Communities

At FurEverHome we believe every animal deserves a caring home. We empower shelters, volunteers, and families to find safe, loving matches without bureaucracy.

We combine human-centered design and local partnerships to reduce shelter overflow, support animal health, and build long-term owner success through education and follow-up support.

How It Works

  1. Search — Browse pets by needs, photos, and temperament.
  2. Connect — Message the shelter or owner and schedule a meet.
  3. Adopt / Support — Complete digital forms, get vet-check support, and finalize adoption.

Safe, Supported, and Transparent

  • Pre-adoption vet screenings for every pet.
  • Secure profiles and verified partner shelters.
  • 30-day adoption support and return policy.
  • Clear fee breakdown — no hidden costs.
